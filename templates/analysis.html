Popular Travel (Local)

Looking at this plot, it can be seen that the slight majority of the points are on the right side of the plot. This might lead one to claim the northern hemisphere has stronger winds than the southern. However, due to the layout of the data it cannot be declaired that the nothern hemisphere is statistically different. I did however take the time to research why the slight increase may have occered in the north. Once again it has to do with the seasons, wind likes to move from areas of higher temperature to areas of lower. Since this data was collected during the northern hemisphere's winter, the concentration of wind is going to be higher in the north rather than the south.
